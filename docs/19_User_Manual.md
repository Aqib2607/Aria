# Aria

## AI-Powered Career Guidance & Learning Roadmap Assistant

**Version:** 1.0

**Audience:** End Users

---

# 1. Introduction

Welcome to **Aria**, your AI-powered career mentor.

Aria helps students, graduates, and self-learners discover suitable career paths, identify skill gaps, generate personalized learning roadmaps, prepare for interviews, and monitor learning progress through artificial intelligence.

---

* Mobile

* Google Chrome

* Safari

A stable internet connection is required for AI-powered features.

# 3. Creating an Account

### Step 1

Open the Aria website.

### Step 2

Click **Register**.

### Step 3

Enter:

* Full Name
* Email Address
* Password
* Confirm Password

### Step 4

Click **Create Account**.

### Step 5

Verify your email address.

---

# 4. Logging In

1. Open the Login page.
2. Enter your email.
3. Enter your password.
4. Click **Login**.

Successful login redirects you to the Dashboard.

---

# 5. Dashboard Overview

The Dashboard displays:

* Welcome Message
* Career Goal
* Learning Progress
* AI Recommendations
* Skill Summary
* Roadmap Progress
* Recent AI Activity
* Notifications

---

# 6. Completing Your Profile

Navigate to:

**Profile → Edit Profile**

Complete:

* Personal Information
* Education
* University
* Degree
* Graduation Year
* Skills
* Interests
* Bio

Click **Save Changes**.

# 7. Selecting a Career Goal

Navigate to:

**Dashboard → Career Goals**

Steps:

1. Browse available careers.
2. Select your preferred career.
3. Save your goal.
4. Set it as your active career (optional).

---

# 8. Adding Skills

Navigate to:

**Dashboard → Skills**

You can:

* Add skills
* Edit proficiency levels
* Remove skills

Keeping your skills updated improves AI recommendations.

# 18. Troubleshooting

## Cannot Log In

Possible causes:

* Incorrect email or password
* Email not verified
* Account suspended

Solution:

* Reset password or contact the administrator.

---

## AI Response Not Generated

Possible causes:

* Internet connection issue
* AI service unavailable
* Request limit exceeded

Solution:

* Retry after a short delay.

---

## Dashboard Not Loading

Possible causes:

* Server maintenance
* Network issues

Solution:

* Refresh the page or try again later.

---

# 19. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Can I have multiple career goals?

Yes. You may create multiple goals, but only one can be active at a time.

---

### Can I regenerate my roadmap?

Yes. Previous roadmap versions are preserved.

---

### Will my AI history be saved?

Yes. All AI interactions are stored in your history unless removed according to system policies.

---

### Can I update my skills later?

Yes. Your profile and skills can be updated at any time.

---
